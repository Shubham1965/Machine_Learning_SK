5. AdaBoost
6. Neural Networks
7. Convolutional Neural Networks
8. Word Embeddings
9. Recurrent Neural Networks
10. Graphical Models
11. Latent Variable Models
12. Generative Adversial Networks (GANs)
13. Variational Autoencoders (VAEs)
